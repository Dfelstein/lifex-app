Life X Score — How it works

Your Life X Score is a single number (0–100) that combines three health pillars from your most recent scans:

  • DEXA Body Composition — body fat %, lean mass, VAT
  • Blood Panel — % of markers in optimal or normal range
  • Metabolic Rate (RMR) — fat-burning % at rest

Each pillar is scored out of 100 and averaged. Missing data (e.g. no blood panel yet) is excluded from the average — it won't penalise your score.

Score ranges: <60 Needs Work · 60–75 Fair · 76–89 Good · 90–100 Elite
