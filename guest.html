Why DEXA vs Online Calculators?
📏Online tools estimate body fat from height & weight alone — error margin of 5–8%. DEXA is accurate to within 1–2%.
🫀Only DEXA measures visceral adipose tissue (VAT) — the fat around your organs that drives disease risk. No calculator can estimate this.
🦴DEXA measures bone mineral density and gives you a T-score — critical for osteoporosis risk, invisible without a scan.
📊Your Life X dashboard tracks changes scan-to-scan so you can see exactly what's working in your training and nutrition.
